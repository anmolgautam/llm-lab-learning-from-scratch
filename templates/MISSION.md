This is the compass. Every teaching decision — what to teach next, how deep to go, what to skip,
which resources to pin — traces back to this file. If a session's work does not serve it, it does
not happen.

no way to say no to anything. "Be hired as an LLM systems engineer" is. "Ship a working inference
server my team uses" is. If you cannot say what changes when you are done, you are not ready to
